What to Look (and Listen) For

Unsure what's invaded your home? These common signs point to a problem that needs a professional solution.

Strange Noises

Scratching, scurrying, gnawing, or thumping sounds in walls, ceilings, or under the floor, especially at night.

Visual Signs

Droppings, nests, chewed wires, damaged vents, or greasy rub marks along walls and baseboards.

Property Damage

Holes in siding, torn up insulation, damaged gardens, or unexplained odors in specific areas of your home.
